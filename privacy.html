Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety are important to EarnWise Africa.

Introduction

EarnWise Africa respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information of visitors who use our website.

Information We Collect

At this time, EarnWise Africa does not require visitors to create accounts or provide personal information to browse our website.

If you contact us in the future, we may receive information such as your name, email address, or message so we can respond to your request.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to questions or feedback.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To provide useful information about online opportunities and digital skills.

Third-Party Links

EarnWise Africa may include links to external websites and platforms. These websites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage visitors to review them before sharing personal information.

Online Safety

We encourage users to protect their personal information, avoid sharing passwords, and be careful when using online services.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as EarnWise Africa grows and adds new features.
