Scam Alerts

Learn how to recognize common online scams and protect yourself while searching for jobs, freelance work, and online earning opportunities.

Common Online Scams

Fake Job Offers

Be cautious of employers who ask you to pay registration fees, training fees, or application charges before hiring you. Legitimate employers rarely ask for payment.

Fake Investment Schemes

Avoid platforms that promise guaranteed profits or extremely high returns with little or no risk. Always research before investing.

Phishing Scams

Never share passwords, one-time verification codes, banking details, or personal information through suspicious emails, messages, or websites.

Fake Giveaways

Scammers often claim you have won a prize and ask you to pay a fee or provide sensitive information to claim it.

Identity Theft

Protect your identity by avoiding the sharing of ID documents or personal information unless you have confirmed that the organization is legitimate.

How to Stay Safe

  • Always research companies before applying.
  • Use official websites and verified contact information.
  • Never pay money for guaranteed employment.
  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
  • Be cautious of offers that sound too good to be true.
  • Keep your devices updated with the latest security software.

Report a Suspicious Opportunity

If you come across a suspicious opportunity, please let us know through our Submit Opportunity page. We review submissions to help keep our community informed.

Our Commitment

EarnWise Africa is committed to promoting safe online practices and sharing educational information about legitimate online opportunities. Although we aim to provide accurate information, users should always conduct their own research before applying for jobs, investing money, or sharing personal information.
