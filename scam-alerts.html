🚨 Scam Alerts

Learn how to identify online scams and protect yourself while searching for opportunities.

Common Online Scams

❌ Fake Job Offers

Be cautious of jobs that ask you to pay money before you can start working. Legitimate employers do not charge application or hiring fees.

❌ Fake Investment Schemes

Be careful with offers that promise guaranteed profits or very high returns in a short time. If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is.

❌ Phishing Messages

Never share your passwords, one-time verification codes, or banking details through email, text messages, or social media.

❌ Fake Giveaways

Scammers may pretend you've won a prize and ask you to pay a fee or provide sensitive information to claim it.

Stay Safe Online

  • ✅ Verify opportunities using official company websites.
  • ✅ Research the company before applying.
  • ✅ Never pay money to get a job.
  • ✅ Protect your passwords and personal information.
  • ✅ Be cautious of unrealistic earning promises.

Our Commitment

EarnWise Africa is committed to sharing trusted information and encouraging safe online practices. While we strive to highlight legitimate opportunities, users should always do their own research before applying or sharing personal information.
