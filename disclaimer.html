Important information about using EarnWise Africa.

Purpose of EarnWise Africa

EarnWise Africa provides information about online opportunities, digital skills, freelancing platforms, remote work, and online safety resources.

Our goal is to help users discover useful information and make informed decisions about online opportunities.

Third-Party Opportunities

Some links on EarnWise Africa lead to external websites and platforms that are not owned or controlled by us.

We encourage users to research and verify every opportunity before creating accounts, sharing personal information, or applying.

No Guarantee of Earnings

EarnWise Africa does not guarantee employment, income, business success, or financial results from any opportunity shared on this website.

Results may vary depending on individual skills, effort, experience, and other factors.

Online Safety

  • Never pay money to someone promising a guaranteed job.
  • Do not share passwords or sensitive personal information.
  • Always use official websites when applying for opportunities.
  • Be careful of offers that seem unrealistic.

Changes to This Disclaimer
