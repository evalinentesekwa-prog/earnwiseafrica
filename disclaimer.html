Important information about using EarnWise Africa.

Purpose of EarnWise Africa

EarnWise Africa provides information about online opportunities, digital skills, freelancing platforms, remote work, and online safety resources.

Our goal is to help users discover useful information and make informed decisions before applying for online opportunities.

Third-Party Websites

EarnWise Africa may share links to external websites, platforms, and resources. These websites are not owned or controlled by EarnWise Africa.

Users should always research and verify opportunities before creating accounts, sharing information, or applying.

No Guarantee of Earnings

EarnWise Africa does not guarantee employment, income, business success, or financial results from any opportunity shared on this website.

Results depend on individual skills, experience, effort, and other factors.

Online Safety

  • Never pay money for a guaranteed job opportunity.
  • Do not share passwords or sensitive personal information.
  • Use official websites when applying for opportunities.
  • Be careful of unrealistic promises.

EarnWise Africa may update this disclaimer as the website grows and new features are added.
